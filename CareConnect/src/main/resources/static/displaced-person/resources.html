Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for emergency housing assistance?

You can apply through our Request Help form, selecting "Shelter & Housing" as your request type. Our team will review your application and contact you within 24 hours.

What documents do I need to apply for financial aid?

You'll typically need identification, proof of residence, and documentation of your losses. Our Financial Aid resource guide provides a complete checklist.

How long does it take to process benefit applications?

Processing times vary by program, but emergency applications are typically processed within 3-5 business days. You'll receive updates throughout the process.
